ABOUT US

The Donut Haus has been producing exceptional donuts in Estes Park since 1975. For 40 years our small donut shop has been hand-crafting donuts using our original recipes that have been passed down from baker to baker.

We take pride in continuing the tradition of producing the highest quality donuts using the freshest ingredients available.

Come experience what generations of locals and tourists have come to love about our donuts!
