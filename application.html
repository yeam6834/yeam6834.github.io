Geometry Dash

Overview

Geometry Dash is a rhythm-based platformer game where timing and precision are key. You must navigate your square character through levels filled with obstacles, and enjoy the music!

How to Play

Take a moment to look at the controls below. Once ready, try to complete the levels! The further levels you go, the more complex they become!

Control Action Description
Click/Tap Spacebar Jump Press to jump over obstacles and pits
Hold Spacebar Fly/Hover Hold to continuously fly when in flight mode
Release Spacebar Stop Flying Release to drop back down when flying
