Gordie Howe

Red Wings Hockey Legend

Gordie Howe is a great example of a hero. Why?

A hero is a role model. Someome who you look up to. To me, an essential quality of a hero is someone who can get the best out of the people around them.

In many ways Gordie Howe is the defintion of a hero. He is a pioneer in the game of hockey and is considered one of the best players of all time. Through his determination and perserverance he inspires me and many others to keep pushing and striving for our dreams. His accountability, toughness and pure talent was a driving force behind his team, where he influenced and pushed his teammates like no one else can.
